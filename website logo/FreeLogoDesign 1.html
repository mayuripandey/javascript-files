Help+
What can I edit? The FreeLogoDesign tool is very complete, you can edit the color, font size, font, modify the form of the logo, add icons, etc. The only real limitation is your imagination.
What are the available sizes for my logo? Various sizes and formats are available. With the free version, the format is PNG 200x200 pixels (px). High resolution formats are: PNG 200x200px, PNG 500x500px, PNG 1000x1000px, PNG 5000x5000px, JPG 5000x5000px, PDF file and vector SVG.
What can I do with my logo? It depends on your needs. With the free version of your logo, you can very easily use it for your website, Facebook logo or an email signature. For a professional use such as having your logo on a business card, on your store’s window or on a vehicle, you should use the high-resolution version.
How much is a logo? We offer 3 options. The first allows you to create your own free logo. The second is a professional use logo with a high resolution for $59 and the third, a complete customized logo service designed by our graphic artists for $199.
In what currency will I be charged? All prices are in USD
Can I edit my logo after downloading it? Yes, you can edit your logo (font, shape, size, color…) and download it again. If you selected the high resolution option, you can also edit it, but there are some restrictions such as adding an icon or logo, for example. However, if you want to add these items, no worries, you will be notified when you perform an action that requires a purchase.
Do I need to create an account to use FreeLogoDesign? No. With FreeLogoDesign, you don’t need to create an account.
Can I upgrade my logo? Yes, if you realize that you are limited by the use of your free logo, you can click on the link sent in the email that contains your logo, and simply select the high resolution option.
What rights do I have on the logo? Can I copyright my logo? The logos are public, but not exclusive, so you can use them for your everyday business needs. With the Free Logo editor, the customization options are numerous so you can create the perfect logo that will stand out for your business. You can copyright the logo, but in this case, please contact the Brand Protection agency in your country to start the process.
Can I contact the support center by phone? To be able to provide you with an affordable experience, we try to avoid costly overheads such as call centers. Therefore, our support is through email and we are happy to receive your questions so do not hesitate. Contact us by going on the contact page.
